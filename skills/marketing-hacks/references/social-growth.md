`instagram-growth`, `audience-building`

### 18. Instagram non-follower audit [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/C5JC-3KiZ53/) · Mar 2024

`instagram-growth`, `audience-building`

**What it does:** Use Instagram's native data download to identify accounts that don't follow you back, without third-party tools.

**How to execute:**
1. Go to your Instagram profile and tap Activity
2. Scroll to 'Download your information' and tap 'Download or transfer information'
3. Select your account, then tap 'Some of your information'
4. Scroll to and select 'Followers and following'
5. Choose 'Download to device', set date range to 'All time', tap 'Create file'
6. Wait for Instagram's email, click 'Download your information', then download the zip
7. Open the zip, navigate to connections > followers_and_following > followers_1
8. Copy all usernames from each followers page (highlight first, shift-click last)
9. Go to comparetwolists.com and paste followers into List A
10. Repeat copying from the 'following' file and paste into List B
11. Click 'Compare two lists' and check 'Only in List B' for non-followers

**Why it works:** Third-party unfollow tools break at high follower counts and risk account flags, while Instagram's own data export works regardless of audience size.

### 66. Hooks drive follower spikes [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/CoAlcn5LIDP/) · Jan 2023

`tiktok-growth`, `hooks`, `audience-building`

**What it does:** Mastering hooks on TikTok can trigger exponential follower growth after a plateau, even with months of prior consistent posting.

**How to execute:**
1. Post consistently for at least 30 days to establish a baseline
2. Study your lowest-performing videos for weak opening 1-3 seconds
3. Test hook variations: question hooks, shock stat hooks, visual pattern interrupts
4. Identify which hook style produces above-average watch time in your niche
5. Double down on that hook format across all new content

**Why it works:** TikTok's algorithm rewards watch-through rate — a strong hook keeps viewers watching, which signals the algorithm to push the video to more people, compounding growth.

`tiktok-growth`, `content-strategy`, `social-media`
