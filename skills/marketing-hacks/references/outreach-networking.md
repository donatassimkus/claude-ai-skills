### 60. Voice message in cold email [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cn69QhYMFQ_/) · Jan 2023

`email-marketing`, `copywriting`, `tools-workflows`

**What it does:** Replace or supplement plain text cold emails with a linked voice recording to stand out in crowded inboxes.

**How to execute:**
1. Go to Reverb.chat
2. Record a short personalized voice message (under 60 seconds)
3. Copy the shareable link
4. Embed or link it inside the email body — no attachment needed
5. Recipient clicks and hears your voice without leaving their email client

**Why it works:** Voice adds personality and perceived effort that plain text cannot, increasing reply rates on cold outreach.

### 77. Cold email domain warm-up [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cp5ExbeDbJ-/) · Mar 2023

`cold-outreach`, `email-marketing`

**What it does:** Use a new dedicated domain and hire a Fiverr freelancer to warm it up over 2-3 weeks before sending cold emails.

**How to execute:**
1. Register a new domain separate from your main business domain
2. Hire a Fiverr specialist for email warm-up services
3. Wait 2-3 weeks for the warm-up process to complete
4. Connect the warmed domain to a mass email platform like Mailshake

**Why it works:** Sending cold email from a fresh, unwarmed domain triggers spam filters — warm-up builds sender reputation so emails land in inbox.

### 94. LinkedIn Coffee Networking Method [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cr36VkcrN1-/) · May 2023

`linkedin`, `networking`, `audience-building`

**What it does:** Accept all LinkedIn connection requests and proactively message 1st and 2nd degree connections for coffee meetings when visiting new cities.

**How to execute:**
1. Set LinkedIn to accept every incoming connection request to maximize your network size
2. When traveling to or arriving in a new city, filter LinkedIn connections by location
3. Message both 1st and 2nd degree connections with a short, casual coffee invitation
4. Keep the message simple and low-pressure (e.g. 'I'm in [city] this week, would you like to grab coffee?')
5. Meet in person, build the relationship, and let introductions to their network happen naturally

**Why it works:** Large weak-tie networks create compounding serendipity: each new connection introduces you to their network, leading to business deals, partnerships, and opportunities you could never have sourced through cold outreach alone.

### 135. LinkedIn-to-WhatsApp outreach hack [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/CuAdKmeLLGt/) · Jun 2023

`outreach`, `linkedin`, `social-selling`

**What it does:** Use a LinkedIn phone number extension to find prospects' numbers, then message them directly on WhatsApp instead of email or InMail.

**How to execute:**
1. Install a browser extension that reveals phone numbers on LinkedIn profiles (e.g. Lusha, ContactOut, or similar)
2. Find your target prospect's LinkedIn profile
3. Use the extension to extract their phone number
4. Open WhatsApp and message them directly at that number
5. For hard-to-reach targets, find someone in their inner circle first, message that person, and ask for an introduction
6. Lead with a strong, concise pitch since WhatsApp messages get read immediately

**Why it works:** WhatsApp messages have near-100% open rates and feel personal, bypassing crowded email inboxes and LinkedIn InMail where response rates are low.
