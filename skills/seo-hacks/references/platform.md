### 21. Instagram manual upload only [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/C6UDO9tCHY3/) · Apr 2024

`instagram-growth`, `instagram-seo`

**What it does:** Post to Instagram manually through the mobile app, never through VPNs or republishing tools with shared IPs, to avoid algorithmic suppression.

**How to execute:**
1. Download your video from Google Drive to your phone
2. Open the Instagram mobile app (not web app)
3. Upload the video directly through the app
4. Toggle on highest quality upload
5. Do not use republishing tools or VPNs with shared IP addresses

**Why it works:** Content uploaded from shared IP addresses (VPNs, republishing tools) performs measurably worse on Instagram.

### 42. Remove Flagged Bot Followers [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/C9b7FnPiuCf/) · Jul 2024

`instagram-growth`, `instagram-seo`

**What it does:** Use Instagram's built-in feature to remove followers flagged for review (bots/fake accounts) to boost engagement rate and reach.

**How to execute:**
1. Go to your Instagram followers list
2. Look for the 'Flagged for review' section in follower management
3. Select and remove all flagged/bot followers in bulk
4. Monitor engagement over the next 1-2 weeks for increased reach

**Why it works:** When posts are shown to bot followers first, they get zero engagement, signaling low quality to the algorithm. Removing bots means posts reach real people first, driving higher engagement rates that trigger wider distribution.

### 72. SSL redirect link for TikTok [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/CnSoNqxJMiU/) · Jan 2023

`social-media`, `tools-workflows`, `funnels`

**What it does:** Create a custom branded redirect URL with SSL so TikTok bio links display cleanly without showing http/https prefix.

**How to execute:**
1. Register a short, branded domain (e.g. YourBrandLink.com)
2. Spin up an Amazon Lightsail WordPress instance
3. SSH into the instance and use Certbot (Let's Encrypt) to generate a free SSL certificate
4. Create a static IP in Lightsail and point your domain DNS A record to it
5. Install a WordPress site redirect plugin (e.g. Simple Website Redirect)
6. Configure the plugin to redirect all traffic to your destination URL while keeping /wp-admin accessible
7. Paste the clean domain (no protocol prefix) into your TikTok bio

**Why it works:** TikTok auto-assumes HTTPS, so a short branded domain with SSL looks clean in bio without protocol clutter, and you control the redirect destination.

### 103. Patience With Short-Form SEO [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DAzTacGCRN1/) · Oct 2024

`youtube-seo`, `analytics`

**What it does:** Do not judge YouTube Shorts performance in the first days or weeks — the algorithm can take hundreds of days to surface content.

**How to execute:**
1. Set a minimum evaluation window of 30-90 days before declaring a Short dead
2. Track delayed performers in a spreadsheet — note upload date and date of first spike
3. Identify patterns in what eventually pops (topic, hook style, thumbnail) to inform future content
4. Never delete old Shorts that underperformed — they are dormant assets, not failures

**Why it works:** YouTube's recommendation algorithm re-evaluates old Shorts when related topics trend or engagement signals improve, giving content a second (or third) life long after posting.

### 115. AI YouTube Thumbnail Pipeline [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DCy3InvC3Sp/) · Nov 2024

`youtube-seo`, `video-production`, `tools-workflows`

**What it does:** Create professional YouTube thumbnails by combining multiple AI tools (Midjourney, ChatGPT, Grok, background remover) in a specific workflow.

**How to execute:**
1. Take a PNG photo of yourself as the base image
2. Run it through an AI upscaler tool with 'face enhance' enabled to sharpen a blurry source photo
3. Open Midjourney's edit tool on the Midjourney website (not Discord)
4. Erase everything except your face/hands and type a prompt describing what to wear (e.g. 'a man wearing a lab coat')
5. Pick the best generated variation and use Midjourney's upscale feature on it
6. Download the upscaled image and remove the background with a one-click background remover
7. Use ChatGPT to write a detailed image generation prompt for the background
8. Feed that prompt into Grok (on X) to generate the background image
9. Composite the foreground cutout and AI background together in Figma

**Why it works:** Produces studio-quality thumbnails without a photographer, studio, or Photoshop skills, which directly impacts YouTube CTR.

### 120. High-frequency Instagram posting strategy [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DFLmBeMCvD1/) · Jan 2025

`instagram-growth`, `instagram-seo`, `content-strategy`

**What it does:** Posting 6-12+ times per day on Instagram accelerates follower growth significantly faster than standard posting cadences.

**How to execute:**
1. Batch-create content in advance — aim for 6-12 pieces per day minimum
2. Schedule posts evenly across the day (e.g. every 2 hours for 12 posts)
3. Do not self-censor or worry about over-posting — the algorithm filters distribution per user
4. Track daily follower gain; increase frequency if growth plateaus
5. Start at 6x/day, scale to 12x/day once content production system is in place

**Why it works:** Instagram's algorithm shows each post only to a subset of followers based on engagement signals, so high post volume increases total reach without spamming any individual follower's feed.

### 123. Trial Reels Repost Loop [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DG_cQseixV6/) · Mar 2025

`instagram-growth`, `instagram-seo`

**What it does:** Repost your best performing reels using Instagram's Trial Reels feature repeatedly to keep generating new followers from non-follower reach.

**How to execute:**
1. Identify reels that already performed well organically (high reach, saves, follows)
2. Repost the top performer as a Trial Reel
3. Wait until the trial reel's reach stalls (typically after ~100 new followers)
4. Repost the same video again as another Trial Reel
5. Repeat the cycle indefinitely since Trial Reels only show to non-followers

**Why it works:** Trial Reels are shown exclusively to non-followers, so each repost reaches a fresh audience that has never seen the content, making proven content perform well repeatedly.
