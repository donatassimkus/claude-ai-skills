# Brand / PR Hacks

### 1. Viral stunt for backlinks [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/C-YxAkOijT3/) · Aug 2024

`backlinks`, `brand`, `content-strategy`

**What it does:** Create a high-budget viral video or stunt that earns massive organic backlinks from authority sites.

**How to execute:**
1. Identify a product or brand launch that needs domain authority and press coverage
2. Invest heavily in one flagship piece of content designed to go viral (video, stunt, spectacle)
3. Distribute the content on high-reach platforms (X, YouTube, TikTok) to maximize shares
4. Let organic press coverage and social sharing generate backlinks from top authority domains
5. Monitor backlink acquisition with Ahrefs or similar and use the authority to rank for target keywords

**Why it works:** A single viral moment can generate thousands of high-authority backlinks that would take years to build through traditional outreach.

### 49. Volume podcast outreach sprint [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cl018PTt_4G/) · Dec 2022

`backlinks`, `outreach`, `digital-pr`

**What it does:** Batch 20+ podcast bookings into a two month sprint to rapidly build backlink volume.

**How to execute:**
1. Set a specific numeric target (e.g. 20 podcasts in 60 days)
2. Build a list of 100+ relevant podcasts accepting guests
3. Create a repeatable pitch template you can personalize quickly
4. Track outreach, responses, and booking status in a spreadsheet
5. Stack multiple recordings per week to hit the target
6. Monitor new backlinks appearing in Ahrefs or Search Console as episodes publish

**Why it works:** Concentrated sprints create a burst of new referring domains in a short window, which signals growing authority to Google faster than sporadic link building.

### 84. Contributor social proof flywheel [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/CpR-qRNM7db/) · Mar 2023

`audience-building`, `social-media`, `backlinks`

**What it does:** Turn guest contributors into unpaid promoters by giving them a credible platform they want to brag about.

**How to execute:**
1. Ensure your site has a verifiable authority metric (e.g. SimilarWeb global rank) contributors can reference
2. After publishing, notify the contributor with a share-ready link
3. Contributors naturally post on LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram about being published
4. Each share sends new visitors to your site, improving traffic metrics and authority scores
5. Higher authority attracts better contributors, restarting the cycle

**Why it works:** People publicly boast about writing for ranked sites, which generates free distribution and raises your domain authority metrics month over month.

### 109. Brand keyword traffic illusion [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DBg4VVJi9su/) · Oct 2024

`google-seo`, `analytics`

**What it does:** A spike in organic traffic may only reflect branded search terms (people searching your name), not actual SEO gains on competitive non-branded keywords.

**How to execute:**
1. Open Google Search Console and filter queries by brand name vs non-brand
2. In Ahrefs/Semrush, check whether ranking keywords contain your brand name
3. Segment traffic reports into branded vs non-branded buckets
4. Only count non-branded keyword growth as real SEO progress
5. Use this analysis on competitors to see if their traffic spike is genuine or brand-driven

**Why it works:** Branded traffic inflates organic numbers but does not indicate ranking power for commercial or informational queries that drive new customer acquisition.

### 111. Buy category-defining generic domains [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DCEtgHXi40i/) · Nov 2024

`domain-strategy`, `brand`

**What it does:** Acquire short generic domains that match the common word people use to describe your product category, positioning your brand as the default.

**How to execute:**
1. Identify the one-word term users naturally use when referring to your product (e.g., 'chat' for ChatGPT)
2. Check availability of that term as a .com domain
3. Purchase or negotiate acquisition even at a premium

5. The domain itself becomes a branding asset that signals category ownership

**Why it works:** Owning the generic category domain makes your brand synonymous with the action, reducing friction and dominating type-in traffic.

### 172. ChatGPT branded query regex filter [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTOWYhhjAOd/) · Jan 2026

`analytics`, `google-seo`, `brand`

**What it does:** Use ChatGPT to generate a regex pattern that filters Google Search Console for all branded search variations.

**How to execute:**
1. Prompt ChatGPT: 'My brand name is [brand]. My URL is [website.com]. Give me regex for Google Search Console to filter only branded searches, include any possible variations.'
2. Copy the regex output from ChatGPT.
3. Open Google Search Console → Performance → Search results.
4. Click 'Add Filter' → Query → 'Filter for Regex' → paste the regex.
5. Click 'More' → Compare → set last 3 months vs previous 3 months.

**Why it works:** Branded search volume is a direct proxy for off-site marketing effectiveness — rising branded clicks confirm that social or content campaigns are building real brand awareness.

### 180. Brand + Alternative PPC targeting [source](https://www.instagram.com/p/DTvzWrcjN19/) · Jan 2026

`paid-ads`, `competitive-seo`, `brand`

**What it does:** Bid on '[competitor brand] + alternative' search queries to intercept users actively looking to switch away from a competitor.

**How to execute:**
1. Open Google Ads and create a new search campaign
2. Add keywords: '[competitor] alternative', '[competitor] alternatives', 'alternative to [competitor]'
3. Write ad copy that positions your product as the superior switch
4. Set match types to exact and phrase to control spend
5. Monitor impression share and adjust bids based on conversion cost

**Why it works:** Users searching '[brand] alternative' have high intent to switch, making them the most qualified prospects you can intercept cheaply before a competitor does.
