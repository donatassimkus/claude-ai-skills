# Sales Script Templates

Generic script templates for every sales scenario. Swap placeholders for your product, company, and offer. These complement the Kill Skills frameworks in the main skill file.

Placeholders: [NAME] = prospect name, [REP] = your name, [COMPANY] = your company, [PRODUCT] = what you sell, [LEAD MAGNET] = the content they opted into, [PROBLEM] = their stated constraint, [GOAL] = their desired outcome, [CATEGORY] = problem label from discovery.

---

Great. I will text that over to you... awesome, well [NAME]... I am excited for you to speak with [CLOSER NAME] on [DAY OF APPOINTMENT]... anything else you need from me?

IF VALUE: Continue the script.

## 3. Inbound Set Phone Script

Use immediately when an inbound lead books a call. Double dial on your first no-call opening.

Hey [NAME]! It is [REP] from [COMPANY] on a recorded line. How is it going?

Just had a couple minutes in between meetings. Excited to talk with you on [DATE] and [TIME]. Did you get the invite for that?

I actually have some time now... would it be crazy if we just had our call now?

Ok no worries, do you have a quick 30 seconds so I can ask a couple questions to be as efficient as possible on our call?

- What type of business do you own?
- What is revenue roughly?
- What has you interested in [PRODUCT]?
- Perfect... by the way did you see that quick video after you booked?
- If not: I will send it over. It will save us time on the call.

**IF YES (they have time now):** Transition into Closing Script.

## 6. Looping Script

**STEP 1: NO MATTER WHAT THEY SAY**

I completely hear you... Do you mind if I ask you a question?

Do you think doing [PRODUCT] is something that can help get closer to [GOAL]?

**STEP 2: ISOLATE THEN FILTER**

IF "Yes I do, but [new objection]":

CONFIRM: Ah... so the main thing is [OBJECTION]?

*Wait for response*

ISOLATE: Other than [OBJECTION]... is there anything else holding you back?

*Wait for response*

Move to the relevant objection section below.

IF "Yeah..." (uncertain tone):

PROBE: Hmm... you do not seem super confident.

*Wait for response*

IF SPECIFIC OBJECTION: Confirm and isolate as above.

IF NO SPECIFIC OBJECTION: Well, what is your main concern?

CONFIRM: Got it... is it more [OBJECTION TYPE #1] or [OBJECTION TYPE #2]?

ISOLATE: So other than [OBJECTION] is anything else holding you back?

IF "Yes I do" (certain tone):

Love it... I am curious... what makes you say that?

*Wait for response*

I agree. Since you are dealing with [PROBLEM]... and we [PROOF/CREDIBILITY]...

It sounds like we can really help... I mean how could we just get this going?

*Wait for response*

ISOLATE: Well, it sounds like [OBJECTION] is the only thing holding you back? Is there anything else?

Mind if I present some possible solutions?

Move to the relevant objection section below.

IF "I'M NOT SURE":

PROBE: Oh... well what is your main concern?

ISOLATE: Got it, outside of [OBJECTION] do you have any other concerns?

Move to the relevant objection section below.

"I am overwhelmed / I have a lot going on right now"

Exactly. That is why being a bit nervous is actually a good thing... because you probably will take it more seriously... And you do see the value?

## 7. BAMFAM Script

Use when you BAMFAM a meeting. Nurtures the appointment between meetings.

**Text #1 (5 min follow up):**

Hey [NAME], so great talking with you. Excited to continue the conversation and potentially do business together. We can help.

(If applicable: Also, here is a quick video that reminded me of you [RELEVANT CONTENT LINK])

**Text #2 (Night before):**

Looking forward to our meeting tomorrow.

**Text #3 (Morning of):**

Talk today.

**If booked more than 3 days out, send value messages:**

1) Hey [NAME], just saw this case study of [SIMILAR COMPANY] who solved [THEIR PROBLEM]. Quick watch: [LINK]

2) [NAME], saw this video from [FOUNDER]. Thought you might enjoy. [LINK]

## 8. Referral Script

Use right after closing and collecting payment, or the day after.

**On the call (post-close):**

Great. We have got you all locked in. Quick question for you. You are the exact type of [customer/business owner] we love to work with. If we could talk to only people like you all day... we would.

Who else do you know who is as successful as you that would benefit from this? I ask because we have a few spots left and I would love to fill it with a couple people you know.

IF YES: You against putting us all in a group text? I can shoot you a message to copy paste if that is easier for you. It just says "[I am going through [PRODUCT]. Thought you might want to check it out. This is [REP NAME]. I will let you guys take it from here.]" Cool?

GET MORE: Great. Anyone else come to mind?

IF NO: Alright. And you know [FOUNDER] would be disappointed if I did not ask... so how about a [PEER] less successful than you? (laugh)

IF STILL NO: Move on.

**Post-sale text (day after):**

Hey [NAME]! Excited to have you on board. Curious, did you know any other [PEERS] who might benefit from [PRODUCT]?

IF YES: Love it. Do you mind putting us in a group chat together? That is typically the easiest way.

IF NO: No worries! Feel free to holler at me when you do.

## 9. No-Show Script

Use after double dialing with no response at scheduled time.

1) Immediately: "Hey [NAME], just tried your cell. Ready to rock?"

2) If no response in 5 min: "I know life is busy... Happy to find another time. I have some openings tomorrow at [TIME] or [TIME]. Either of those work to reschedule?"

3) Add to your outbound block at end of day if no response.

## 10. Pipeline Phone and Text Script

Use when: (1) waiting on a condition to close (payday, card, partner approval) and failed to get a BAMFAM, or (2) prospect has ghosted.

Start by calling. If no answer, transition to text. Goal: close the deal or agree on a timeline.

**Phone script:**

Hey [NAME], it is [REP] from [COMPANY] calling from a recorded line... how have you been?

Great, just had a few minutes, wanted to touch base around [OBSTACLE PREVENTING THE DEAL FROM MOVING FORWARD]. How is it going there?

Transition into relevant objection looping.

**Text sequence:**

**Step 0 (5 min follow up, after first call):**

Hey [NAME], so great talking with you. Excited to continue the conversation and potentially do business together. We can help.

**Step 1 (Value messages):**

- Hey [NAME], just saw this case study of [SIMILAR COMPANY] who solved [THEIR PROBLEM]. Quick watch: [LINK]
- [NAME], remember you mentioned struggling with [PROBLEM]? One of our clients just shared this: [LINK]
- Quick heads up [NAME], found a video that directly addresses [PROBLEM]. Thought you would want to see this before we reconnect: [LINK]

**Step 2 (Check-ins):**

- Hey [NAME], what did you think about [the proposal/content]?
- [NAME], how are you feeling about [next steps/decision]?
- Hey [NAME], what questions came up when you reviewed [RESOURCE]?
- How are you feeling since our last call?

**Step 3 (Bumping):**

- [NAME]?
- [NAME], any questions around this?
- [NAME], should I assume this is not a priority right now?
- Hey [NAME], different question: what is the biggest challenge on your plate this week?
- [NAME], taking a step back, is [original topic] still relevant or have priorities shifted?

**Step 4 (Re-offer / Re-engage):**

- [NAME], just had spots open up for [DATES]. Want first dibs before I announce it?
- Hey [NAME], update on [THEIR SITUATION]: we just solved this exact problem for [SIMILAR COMPANY]. Want the details?
- [NAME], been thinking about your [PROBLEM]. Got 5 minutes for a quick solution I think you will love?
