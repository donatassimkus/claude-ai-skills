Copy [`INSTALL-PROMPT.md`](INSTALL-PROMPT.md) and paste it into your AI agent (Claude Code, Cursor, or similar). It installs the skill files unchanged, asks one question about your business shape, then runs a Google Business Profile audit on your own business. No accounts, no keys.
